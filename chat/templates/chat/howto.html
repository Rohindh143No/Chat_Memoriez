How to Use MEMORIEZ

1

Open WhatsApp and go to any chat.

2

Click on the 3 dots in the top right corner.

3

Select "Export Chat" without Media.

4

The chat will be exported as a ZIP file. Extract it to a folder.

5

Go to the MEMORIEZ website and select the extracted ZIP file.

6

Click "Generate" to process your file.

7

After processing, you can either view or download your chat file.
